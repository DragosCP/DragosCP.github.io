GPR PRO: Data Management at Nuclear Plant

Case study on data analysis and process optimization at Hinkley Point Nuclear Plant.

Project Background

At Hinkley Point Nuclear Plant, I employed a strategic approach to identify gaps in project coordination as a Data Manager. The project involved managing and analyzing Ground Penetration Radar (GPR) scans of 10,000 concrete blocks to identify structural defects.

When I joined, the project was approximately 60-70% delivered, but roughly 60% of those deliverables were classified as "bad" results, leading to significant project delays.

Solutions & Impact

  • Created transparent documentation system for tracking scan processing status and issues
  • Developed automation scripts that reduced data processing time by 33%
  • Identified quality issues in data processing through statistical analysis
  • Implemented A/B testing showing that 50% of "bad" results were inaccurate
  • Reduced percentage of defective foundations from 60% to 32%
  • Saved client approximately 2 years of remedial work and millions in project costs
